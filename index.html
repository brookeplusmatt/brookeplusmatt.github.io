TO OUR FAMILY & FRIENDS TRAVELING FROM OUT OF TOWN


The closest airport is BWI Thurgood Marshall. Cab fare to Baltimore's inner harbor costs around $30, but you can also take the light rail or MARC train into downtown.


For getting around inside the city, we recommend Lyft, a ridesharing service that we use often. Use our referral code MATT2950 for a free $10 ride!


We've reserved a block of rooms at the Hilton Garden Inn Baltimore Inner Harbor. If you book a room with our group code, you'll get a special discounted rate and brunch courtesy of Chris and Pam Pazoles (Matt's parents) on Sunday morning.* Call 800-445-8667 to make a reservation using our group code "APW", or use our group's online reservation site here.


*Please feel free to join us at the hotel for brunch, even if you choose to stay elsewhere!
